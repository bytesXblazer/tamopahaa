Our Vision

The dream is of making things work as it should and unblemished interpretation of every facet of life. And the belief that this is the key to make life simpler, productive and meaningful with technology and innovations aiding this process and thus contribute to make earth a better happy place for all its beings – human, other living things and nature.

This is not a single dimensional problem which we work out a project plan to be done by a date. It is more of tending towards, make small differences and the accumulated effect will be felt.

Tamopahaa to be a place to solve problems across various facets of living and life, using technology and innovation. But the icing on the cake is to attempt this by being a stickler to the original definitions of every term used say – business, profit, loss, demand, supply, market , sales, etc. and doing the job with no short cuts.
